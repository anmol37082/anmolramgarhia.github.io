Why Fusion Energy?

Limitless Energy

Fusion fuel is virtually inexhaustible, with enough deuterium in seawater to power civilization for billions of years.

Zero Emissions

Fusion produces no greenhouse gases or air pollution, making it the ultimate clean energy solution.

Inherent Safety

Fusion reactions can't run away - any disturbance causes the plasma to cool and the reaction to stop naturally.

Baseload Power

Fusion provides consistent, reliable power 24/7 regardless of weather conditions or time of day.
